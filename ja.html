The Tien Shan Mountains hide in their heights a placer of the most beautiful lakes in Asia, the most grandiose of which is Lake Sary-Chelek. The lake is located at an altitude of 1873 m, its length is 7500 m, width is 2280 m, and the depth reaches 234 m. You will go through picturesque passes, deep gorges, along turbulent river beds, and many smaller lakes, which look like a precious chain of transparent diamonds from the from heights.
