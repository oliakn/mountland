ABOUT US

The Mountland company is mainly focused on tourism, mountaineering, horse walks, and horse racing. Moreover on a cultural and entertaining center as a visit to the cult places of Kyrgyzstan.

And those who prefer a special drive can have a tour on mountain roads in off-road vehicles.

Costumers are provided with accommodation in the best hotels, and if you want to feel the spirit of a Kyrgyz nomad, rest in the wild nature in nomad yurts is waiting for you.
